## Workflow

1. First, think through the problem in the prompt. Read the codebase and write a plan in tasks/todo.md
2. The plan should be a checklist of todo items.
3. Then, complete the todos one by one, marking them off as you go.
4. At every step, give me a high-level explanation of what you changed.
5. Keep every change simple and minimal. Avoid big rewrites.
6. At the end, add a review section in todo.md summarizing the changes.
